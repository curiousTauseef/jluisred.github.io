About me

I'm a PhD candidate at Eurecom Institute in France, in the last year before my thesis defense. I obtained with honors my Master's thesis in Computer Science at the University of Extremadura, in Spain. I have been involved in EU projects like LinkedTV, MediaMixer and NexGenTV, and my research interests include Semantic Web, Multimedia Technologies and Knowledge Representation focusing on the annotation of News Stories.
